About

Finbar is an Irish programmer, web designer and video editor. He's been coding ever since he was 7 years old, when he took a CTYI course. In 2017, he won 2nd place in his category for Cyberstick, a website allowing users to share files anonymously and he won 3rd place in the All Ireland Programming Olympiad

Finbar is fluent in a wide range of computer languages:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Javascript
  • C++
  • Node.js
  • Python
  • C

Finbar has a wide range of competencies in web design wth both individual and corporate clients.

Finbar has experience in the field of video editing, with particular competence in recording and editing audition tapes for entertainers.

Finbar believes that a good programmer never stops learning. The truly ambitious in this field approach every project as a new learning experience. This is the philosphy he brings to each of his endeavours.

Finbar is fluent in English and Italian. He has a strong work ethic, and sense of commitment to his work. He sees every project through from start to finish with impeccable attention to detail.
